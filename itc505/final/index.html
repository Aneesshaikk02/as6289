Addendum: The Making of "Revenge of Lights Out"

Welcome to "Revenge of Lights Out," a modern twist on the classic Lights Out game. In this section, I'll share some insights into the development process and the creative decisions made along the way.

Design Philosophy:

- Color Scheme: I opted for a soothing light green background to evoke a sense of calmness and concentration for players.
- Layout: Utilizing flexbox, I centered the game container both vertically and horizontally for a polished presentation across devices.

Exciting Features:

- Timer Challenge: Challenge yourself against the clock with our integrated timer that starts upon game initiation and stops upon successful completion.
- New Game Button: Want to reset the board and try again? Simply hit the "New Game" button for a fresh start.

Overcoming Obstacles:

- Styling Dilemmas: Striking a balance between aesthetics and responsiveness posed challenges. Through meticulous use of flexbox and color palettes, we achieved a harmonious design.
- Timer Implementation: Crafting a reliable timer system required meticulous interval management. Our startTimer and resetTimer functions ensure accurate timing throughout gameplay.
- User Interaction: Ensuring intuitive user controls while maintaining game integrity was paramount. The "Start Game" and "New Game" buttons provide seamless interaction for players of all levels.

Insights and Reflections:

- Modular Coding: Employing modular functions enhanced code readability and ease of maintenance.
- Iterative Development: Iterative testing and refinement were pivotal in crafting a seamless user experience.
- Community Feedback: Valuable insights from user feedback drove continuous improvements and optimizations.
